I use CSV (Comma Separated, to be specific) mode frequently, but most of my work is done in Normal mode. I don’t have the CSV toolbar enabled to save on screen space. I just noticed today that when I am in Comma Separated mode and switch to Normal mode, the CSV toolbar remains open (though the Cell toolbar does close since the option is set to do that). Admittedly it’s a minor annoyance, but it would be nice to have the option to automatically close the CSV toolbar when switching to Normal mode (perhaps make it a new option to check on/off in the CSV Options portion of Customize CSV Toolbar?). Thanks for your consideration.
